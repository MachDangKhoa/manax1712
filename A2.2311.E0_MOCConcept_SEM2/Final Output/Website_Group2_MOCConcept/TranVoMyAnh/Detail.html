Product Description

"Dil" is the story of a day just after the rain, about the awakening of the ancient forest after a long sleep, carrying with it the moist and wild atmosphere. With every step on the damp leaves, you can feel the fresh, green breath of moss mingling with the scent of wet earth, bringing a sense of renewal, like the forest's deep sigh after the first rain of the season. As the giants gently tremble, shaking off the heavy droplets from their leaves and stretching to greet the returning sunlight, the soft wood scent gradually becomes dry and strong again, restoring the forest's distinctive fragrance—a scent both rustic and sacred.

Fragrance notes: Sandalwood, moss, cedarwood, coriander, lime, and ginger.
